Privacy Architecture
Encrypted
by design.
Your thoughts are the most sensitive data you have. Mystacy treats them accordingly. Client-side AES-256-GCM encryption means your data is encrypted in your browser before it ever leaves your device. The server — and the AI — only ever sees ciphertext.
Client-side AES-256-GCM — encrypted before it leaves your browser
Supabase stores only ciphertext — no plaintext in the database
AI context is decrypted browser-side before API calls
Zero knowledge architecture — we cannot read your data
HIPAA-considered design for medical professionals
